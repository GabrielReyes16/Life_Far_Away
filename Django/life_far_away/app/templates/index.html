What is an exoplanet?

An exoplanet, also known as an extrasolar planet, is a planet located outside our Solar System. These planets orbit stars other than our Sun. Scientists discover and study exoplanets to understand the diversity of planetary systems in the universe and explore the possibility of habitable worlds beyond our own.

What characteristics make an Exoplanet habitable?

  • Type of Star
  • Category
  • Atmosphere
  • Surface
  • Water Availability
